# Comment Conspiracy - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 20, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Comment Conspiracy ("the Game") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our game on Reddit.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information from Reddit
When you play the Game, we receive your:
- Reddit User ID (anonymous identifier)
- Reddit Username (for leaderboards and contributions)

### 2.2 Game Data
We collect and store:
- Your game progress (streak, accuracy, total games played)
- Your daily guesses and results
- Achievements you've unlocked
- Contributions you've submitted (if any)
- Votes you've cast on contributions (if any)

### 2.3 Aggregate Statistics
We collect anonymous, aggregate data including:
- Total number of players per puzzle
- Distribution of guesses across comments
- Overall accuracy rates by difficulty

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Game functionality
- Track your progress and streaks
- Display leaderboards
- Credit contributions used in puzzles
- Improve the Game experience
- Generate anonymous usage statistics

## 4. Data Storage

All game data is stored using Reddit's Devvit Redis service, which is subject to Reddit's data policies. Data is stored on Reddit's servers and is associated with your Reddit account.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share:
- Your username on public leaderboards (if you rank highly)
- Your username with contributions you've submitted
- Anonymous, aggregate statistics publicly

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as:
- You continue to play the Game
- The Game remains active on Reddit
- Required by Reddit's platform policies

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- View your game statistics within the Game
- Stop playing at any time
- Request deletion of your data (contact us via modmail)

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Reddit's age requirements apply.

## 9. Third-Party Services

The Game operates on Reddit's platform and is subject to:
- [Reddit's Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- [Reddit's User Agreement](https://www.redditinc.com/policies/user-agreement)

## 10. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the subreddit or in-game announcements.

## 12. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request data deletion, please:
- Send a modmail to the subreddit where the Game is hosted
- Open an issue on our GitHub repository

## 13. Reddit Platform

This Game is built on Reddit's Devvit platform. By using the Game, you also agree to Reddit's platform terms and privacy practices.

By playing Comment Conspiracy, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
